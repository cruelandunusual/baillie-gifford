## About the department  

The Film and Photography Team is an award-winning, multi-faceted, full-service in house resource creating engaging content for the firm’s audiences, internal and external, around the globe. The team has a broad range of expertise and experience and draw upon these to produce impactful films, animations, stills content and campaigns for the benefit of Baillie Gifford and its clients. The team collaborate with a range of other in-house teams and stakeholders as well as external suppliers and freelancers to deliver engaging and impactful media.

## Responsibilities:  
Support the Post Production Manager, work closely with them to achieve the goals of the post-production area, team and Firm, carrying out work as delegated by them  
Follow the post-production workflow processes to the standards required by the Firm, maintaining accurate filing systems and update workflow management software.  
Where required, take a brief to grasp the team’s requirements and specifications, following the sign off of the concept by the client. Advise the Post Production Manager and Creative Producer of any potential post-production issues apparent with the agreed creative  
<span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;Attend shoots&nbsp;</span> (both in-house and on location ) taking edit notes. Ingest footage from shoots. Log/input uncut rushes and sound, and synchronising and storing them into files on the system to the required standard  
Review the raw material and compose the edit from brief, treatment and shooting notes in an NLE application (<span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;Adobe Premiere Pro&nbsp;</span>).  
<span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;Create motion graphics&nbsp;</span> to enhance live action video where needed (Adobe Premier Pro & <span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;After Effects&nbsp;</span>)  
Suggest and/or select <span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;music/soundtrack&nbsp;</span>, <span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;B-roll footage&nbsp;</span> etc, as appropriate  
Consult with stakeholders and clients to continue developing the cut, incorporating feedback, to make edit decisions to move the work through to final cut stage  
When the cut is finalised, <span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;grade the footage&nbsp;</span>, <span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;sweeten the audio&nbsp;</span> and <span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;purchase any B-roll&nbsp;</span> etc required. Add in final graphical content and liaise with colleagues regarding <span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;risk warnings&nbsp;</span>, distribution and <span style="font-family: 'Courier';color:#000;background-color:#ffd966;">&nbsp;deliverables&nbsp;</span>
